The Power of Speech

Welcome to the Orator Club, the college's premier organization dedicated to fostering *public speaking, **leadership, and **effective communication* skills. We provide a supportive, fun environment for members to conquer their fear of speaking and master the art of persuasion.

What We Focus On

We build confidence through practice and structured feedback:

  • *Impromptu Speaking* (Table Topics)
  • *Speech Preparation* and Delivery Techniques
  • *Active Listening* and Constructive Feedback
  • Body Language and Vocal Variety Training
